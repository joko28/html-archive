After a few weeks, he got to the most magical place he has come across so far, the Enchanted Forest. It next to the ocean with a white sand beach connecting the two. The water is bright saturated blue with white foam between the water and the beach creating this curvature active pattern. The forest is so entrancing that he camped out there for almost a week because he enjoyed waking up to the sun beaming through the trees hitting his tent. When he goes out for a swim in the ocean, he is refreshed and ready for a new day.

Lake Emerald is the largest Alpine lake in North America. It is 1,645 feet deep, 22 miles long, 12 miles wide with 75 miles of shoreline. It is the second deepest lake in America, next to Oregon’s Crater Lake. Eight million people live less than a half day’s drive from the Basin.
