Steve has been working the same corporate job since he finished his Undergraduate at SJSU. It's the same old and boring life of traveling to and from work. ONLY to go home and do more work. His life is boring and uneventful. He's only ever stayed in the city, and never ventured out beyond the city he lives in now along the west coast. As he waits for his train, he thinks about the things he can possibly do; asking himself "What else is out there" than the mundane life he leads now.

On a whim, he decides to quit his job and travel because he wants to properly live his life and experience the outside world.
