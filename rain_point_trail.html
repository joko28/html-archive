Rain Point Trail is a 2.4 KM in length and takes 1-2 hours to complete (one way). It has amazing view of the violet meadow, bring your camera! This trail is rated as easy so it is ideal for the novice hiker.

REVIEWS/COMMENTS

    Loura Pope ⭐⭐⭐

         I did this hike from Kole Cove to the tip of the mountain and found it pretty easy. I started the hike later in the day than I should have, so I was rushing to make sure I didn't end up hiking in the dark. It has a few difficult inclines! Once you get to the highest point of the trail, there are gorgeous sweeping views of the valley! It's very pretty though.

    Steve S. ⭐⭐⭐⭐
